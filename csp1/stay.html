Where to Stay

Hotels will always be there for you when you need them. But here at VSOM, we are fans of Airbnb. In using Airbnb, we like staying with Superhosts. Superhosts are people who have opened up their homes for travellers and have a good track record of consistently providing clean, safe, and truly accommodating homes wherever you go in the world. In case a host still has not reached Superhost stature, because frankly that's quite a feat, VSOM still makes sure the home being offered has a lot of good honest reviews. Below you will find our favorite Airbnb properties here and abroad so that you get an idea how meticulous we choose where to sleep and relax, even for our own vacays.

Taichung, Taiwan - Girly Chic

Pros: Free In-House Wifi, Private Entrance (No Curfew), Host speaks English, Host can cook breakfast for a small fee
