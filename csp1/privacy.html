VSOM - Privacy Policy

How does VSOM use personal information?

VSOM uses the personal information it collects from you to help it understand your needs and to help it provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:

  • To assess what special flight offers and services we can offer you;
  • To process your request on purchasing our product(s) and service(s) and confirm your travel arrangements;
  • To improve our products and services;
  • For our own marketing purposes, such as sending you updates on our latest offers and promotions. When you provide personal information online, you may indicate that you do not want to receive direct marketing materials from VSOM or any other company.
  • We may contact you using these details to remind you to complete your booking and/or offer our assistance (in case, for instance, you were suffering from technical difficulties).

Does VSOM share my personal information with anyone else?

VSOM may send personally identifiable information about you to other companies or people when:

  • We have your consent to share the information with our affiliates and with selected marketing partners to enable them to send you promotional materials about products or services.
  • We need to share your information to provide the service, product or information you have requested, these companies do not have the right to use your personal information for any purpose other than to provide the service, product or information you have requested from VSOM; or
  • We are legally obliged to provide personal information and booking details to governmental authorities, including customs, Court, and immigration officials. We may share information in the aggregate to assess marketing and sales performance.

Data Security

VSOM is committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access to, or disclosure of, any of your personal information, VSOM has put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects. In order to protect the security of your data, VSOM uses encryption technology when collecting or transferring sensitive personal data such as credit card information.
