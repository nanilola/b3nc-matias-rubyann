  • Have you ever wanted to travel but cannot find the time to research about destinations, create itineraries, book flights, accommodations, and nearby day trips?

  • Have you taken one look at at their offered itineraries and found them too jam-packed for one day's worth of your energy?
  • Have you felt even the slightest doubt as to the cleanliness or safety of the accommodation or tours they might book for you?

If you answered yes to any or all of these questions, well then, VSOM is for you.

Vacay State of Mind or VSOM is a service created for like-minded travellers who wish they can ask someone else to create their travel plans AS IF they made it themselves. As human beings, we are creatures of comfort. But everyone has a different idea of what works and what doesn't, so why should you settle for standard travel packages in which the same itinerary is offered to all? Let VSOM create that balanced travel plan that answers to as many of your needs within your budget (or non-budget? *wink wink*).
